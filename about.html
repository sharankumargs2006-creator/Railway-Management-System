About Indian Railways Project

This project is a modern web-based Railway Reservation System designed to provide a seamless and user-friendly experience for booking train tickets, checking PNR status, and managing reservations. Our goal is to make railway travel easier, faster, and more accessible for everyone.

Features include:

  • Online ticket booking
  • PNR status checking
  • Train schedules
  • User registration and login
  • Admin management
